This file describe how to take screenshot fast.

While conversation you will frequently share screenshot with other members.

To me the fastest way to take screenshot:

There is a button named "print screen" or "prnt scrn" or "prt sc" in the keyborad.

[In laptop keyborad you may need to press fn button with print screen button.]

Just press that and in facebook messenger press control + V.

You should see the screenshot in the messenger.

That's it.
